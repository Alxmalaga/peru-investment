Read The Story Behind
Our Success

PMP Investment S.A.C operates as a specialized mining investment promotion center headquartered in Lima, Peru, delivering expert guidance on strategic and financial matters to domestic and international clients across Peru's mining sector.

We recognize that Peru's mining sector is attracting unprecedented levels of global capital investment. Our core focus lies in crafting customized solutions through strategic analysis and balanced funding approaches, combining local and international resources to align with our clients' objectives.

We specialize in mining investment promotion and organizational development, delivering strategic advisory services to tackle complex challenges. Through comprehensive solutions, we enable our clients to effectively manage and thrive in Peru's dynamic mining capital markets.

Expert Advisory
Services.

Specialized consultation in mining operations, metallurgy, and investment opportunities with our international expert network.

International
Network.

Connecting international investors with premium mining opportunities through our strategic partnerships and business center.

Complete Mining
Solutions.

From legal and financial advisory to technological and commercial guidance for copper, silver and gold mining investments.

WHY US?

We have more than
ten years of experience

PMP Investment S.A.C has more than a decade of experience in Peruvian mining investment promotion, connecting global investors with exceptional opportunities while ensuring sustainable and responsible mining practices.
